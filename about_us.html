About Us - TraceBack

TraceBack was born from a personal experience — one that resonates with countless women around the world: the fear of simply walking down the street.

So many times, I’ve walked through certain areas already anticipating what would happen. A whistle, a comment, someone following me. And even with that anticipation, there was often nothing I could do — except walk faster and hope it wouldn't escalate.

That sense of daily vulnerability sparked the idea behind TraceBack: a collaborative map where women and community members can mark locations where they’ve experienced or witnessed harassment or threats.

By turning these invisible experiences into visible data, TraceBack empowers people to make informed decisions about their surroundings. But more importantly, it gives voice to a reality that is often ignored — and transforms it into something concrete. With enough reports and visual evidence, it becomes easier to demand change, raise awareness, and pressure authorities to take action where it's needed most.

We believe that every person deserves to walk freely, without fear. And through TraceBack, we aim to create a platform that not only informs — but protects, connects, and amplifies the voices that have been silenced for too long.
